Fluffy Pancakes

Home

Description

These fluffy pancakes are light, airy, and perfect for a delicious breakfast. Serve with your favorite syrup, fruit, or a pat of butter!

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 1/4 cups milk
  • 1 egg
  • 3 tablespoons melted butter

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, sift together the flour, baking powder, sugar, and salt.
  2. Make a well in the center and pour in the milk, egg, and melted butter. Mix until smooth.
  3. Heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium-high heat.
  4. Pour or scoop the batter onto the griddle, using about 1/4 cup for each pancake.
  5. Brown on both sides and serve hot with your favorite toppings.
