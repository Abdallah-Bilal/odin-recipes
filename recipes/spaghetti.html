Classic Spaghetti

Home

Description

This classic spaghetti recipe features a simple, flavorful tomato sauce served over perfectly cooked pasta. It's a comforting dish that's quick and easy to make for any night of the week.

Ingredients

  • 12 ounces spaghetti noodles
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 (28-ounce) can crushed tomatoes
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon red pepper flakes (optional)
  • 1 tablespoon sugar (optional, to taste)
  • Freshly grated Parmesan cheese, for serving
  • Fresh basil, for garnish (optional)

Instructions

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Cook spaghetti according to package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. Meanwhile, heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add garlic and saut√© for 1 minute, until fragrant.
  3. Add crushed tomatoes, oregano, basil, salt, pepper, and red pepper flakes. Stir to combine.
  4. Simmer sauce for 15-20 minutes, stirring occasionally. Add sugar if desired to balance acidity.
  5. Toss cooked spaghetti with the sauce until well coated.
  6. Serve hot, topped with Parmesan cheese and fresh basil if desired.
